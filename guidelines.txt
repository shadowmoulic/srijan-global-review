SRIJAN — Full Author Guidelines (V1, 2025)
Aims & Scope
SRIJAN: Global Review of Arts, Science & Humanities publishes original scholarship across Humanities, Social Sciences, Interdisciplinary
Studies, and Performance/Theatre Studies.
Article Types
• Research Article: 3,000–6,000 words
• Review Article: 2,500–5,000 words
• Short Communication / Notes: 1,500–2,500 words
• Book Review: 1,000–1,800 words
Formatting Requirements
• Authors must use the SRIJAN: Global Review of Arts, Science & Humanities standard manuscript template.
• Font: Times New Roman, 12 pt; spacing 1.5; margins 1 inch; justified text.
• Headings must follow hierarchy (H1 > H2 > H3).
• Abstract: 150–250 words + 4–6 keywords.
• Citation Style: MLA 9th (preferred) OR APA 7th — maintain consistency.
File Submission
• Upload ONE PDF for peer review (must NOT contain author identity inside manuscript).
• Upload separate Title Page (DOCX) including:
 - Title of article
 - Author name(s)
 - Affiliation(s)
 - Email(s)
 - ORCID (optional)
• Insert figures/tables where first cited WITH captions.
• For high‑resolution figures (if needed): PNG/JPEG 300 dpi submitted separately.
Ethics & Academic Integrity
• Only original & unpublished work accepted.
• Turnitin similarity preferred < 10%.
• Proper permissions required for copyrighted images/data.
• Any conflict of interest or funding support must be declared.
Peer Review Policy
• Double‑blind review.
• Desk screening: 7–10 days.
• Review period: 3–5 weeks (approximate).
• Revision cycle: 2–4 weeks.
Open Access & Rights
• Authors retain copyright.
• Journal receives non‑exclusive rights to publish & archive.
• Articles are accessible open access on journal site.
File Naming Rules
• TitlePage_Surname_FirstWordOfTitle.docx
• Manuscript_Blind_FirstWordOfTitle.pdf
• Figures_FirstWordOfTitle_Fig1.png (etc.)
