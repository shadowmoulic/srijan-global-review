Privacy Policy

How SRIJAN: Global Review of Arts, Science & Humanities protects and safeguards your personal data and academic contributions

1. Information We Collect

SRIJAN: Global Review of Arts, Science & Humanities collects personal data necessary for the publication and peer-review process. This includes author names, institutional affiliations, academic credentials, email addresses, and ORCID identifiers provided during manuscript submission or reviewer application.

2. Use of Personal Data

The information collected is strictly utilized for:

  • Managing the double-blind peer-review workflow.
  • Communicating editorial decisions and proofreading verification.
  • Indexing and archiving published manuscripts in global digital repositories.
  • Maintaining verifiable records for academic indexing bodies.

3. Data Protection and Confidentiality

We employ stringent organizational and technical measures to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of personal data. Under no circumstances do we sell, lease, or distribute author or reviewer details to third-party marketing entities.

4. Cookies and Analytics

Our web platform utilizes essential functional cookies to ensure smooth website operation and navigation across academic volumes. We do not track users across external web platforms.

5. Your Rights

Registered authors and reviewers have the right to request updates or corrections to their contact details. Note that once an article is published under the Open Access framework, author metadata remains permanently archived to preserve the academic integrity of citation indexes.

6. Inquiries

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy practices or data handling policies, please reach out to our editorial office at srijan.globalreview@gmail.com.
